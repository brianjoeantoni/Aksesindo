ABOUT US

  • About Us

Established in 2010, PT Accessindo Buana Perkasa focuses on network controller technology solutions and electronic security systems. Accessindo offers electronic security system solutions for various kinds of industries, both small and large scale, include office buildings, apartment buildings, residences, and shophouses.

Accessindo independantly produces the IAX series, which is a network controller product that can be connected to advanced technology readers. Apart from that, the IAX series can control hardware such as smart elevators, conventional elevators, barrier gates, speed gates, and room or emergency doors.

Accessindo also develops L-Intelligence Access (LIA) which functions as the access controller management of IAX network controller device. Apart from LIA, accessindo also develops the Electronic Visitor Application software module and L-Visual Integration Platform.

With experience and competencency, Accessindo also develops custom electronic security systems that are reliable and trustworthy according to client needs.
